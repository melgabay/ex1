Ingredient

  • Pasta
  • Tomato
  • Origano
  • Basilic

Preparation

  1. Put the pasta (preference to spaggethi) on the pan with hot water to 9 minutes.
  2. Put the tomato until this look like to the puree.
  3. Put the origano, the basilic, the salt and the peper.
  4. Blend the pasta with the sauce
  5. Eat hot and BON APPETIT!
